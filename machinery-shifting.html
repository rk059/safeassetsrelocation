About This Service

Machinery Shifting Services in India

Shifting heavy machinery requires far more than brute force — it demands expert rigging, precise load calculations and specialised equipment. Our machinery relocation team includes certified rigging professionals with experience moving CNC machines, printing presses, turbines, compressors, packaging lines, and other heavy industrial equipment safely across any distance.

What's Included

  • Certified rigging engineers for load calculation and planning
  • Hydraulic jack systems for controlled lowering and lifting
  • Specialised skidding and roller systems for floor moves
  • Custom timber cribbing and seafreight crating
  • Machine decommissioning, cleaning and recommissioning
  • Route surveys for oversize/overweight loads
  • Third-party insurance for high-value machinery
  • Compliance with OSHA and Indian safety standards
