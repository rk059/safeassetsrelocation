2009 Where It All Began
Our Story

15 Years of Moving Lives Forward

Founded in 2009 in Bangalore by a team of logistics professionals who believed that relocation could be done better — more carefully, more transparently, and more humanely — Safe Assets Relocation has grown from a 5-person team serving Bangalore to a 300+ strong organization operating across 50 Indian cities.

We have moved over 10,000 families and 2,000+ businesses. Each move has strengthened our resolve to be the company that people trust with their most valuable possessions — their memories, their livelihoods, their homes.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make and every move we execute.

🛡️

Safety First

Every packing decision, every route choice, every loading action is evaluated with one priority: keeping your belongings safe.

💎

Transparency

No hidden fees. No surprises. We provide detailed, itemised quotes and keep you informed at every stage of your move.

❤️

Care & Empathy

We understand that a relocation is more than moving objects — it's moving people's lives. We treat every client's belongings as our own.

⚡

Excellence

We set high standards and meet them consistently — whether it's the quality of our packaging materials or our punctuality on move day.

🤝

Partnership

Your relocation is a partnership. We work with you — listening, planning and adapting — to ensure the outcome exceeds your expectations.

🌱

Sustainability

We use recyclable packing materials and optimise vehicle routes to reduce our carbon footprint on every single move.
